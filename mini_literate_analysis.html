About the data

This data set includes diverse measurements of seawater chemistry along with supporting conductivity, temperature, and depth (CTD) data from the Arctic Ocean near the North Pole. This data was downloaded from the Arctic Data Centre

phos <- mean(bg_chem$P)

Calculate the mean readfield ratio

ratio <- (nitrate + nitrite + amm)/phos
